How it works ?

This website was built by three undeergraduate students from the École Nationale des Ponts et Chaussées (ENPC) in order to price options given a set of parameters, and the model in which the user wants to price the option.

To utilize it, the user has to fill the following inputs:
- Stock: name of the underlying asset;
- Model: the model to be used to price the asset (e.g. Black-Scholes);
- Order: the type of order (Call or Put);
- S0: current price of the underlying asset;
- K: the strike of the option;
- T: the maturity of the option;
- r: Interest Rate for the model;

- $\sigma$: volatility of the model; - $\epsilon$: the precision of the computed price;

The outputs are the following:
- price of the option;
- computation time;
- the bounds of the confidence interval, considering the precision input.
